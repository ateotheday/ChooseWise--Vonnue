# Career Decision Framework

## Common Decisions
- Government vs private job
- Switch career domain
- Promotion vs work-life balance
- Startup vs stable job

## Key Criteria

### Job Stability
Risk of layoffs, long-term security.

### Salary Growth
Short-term vs long-term income potential.

### Work Pressure
Stress levels, deadlines, performance expectations.

### Growth Opportunities
Learning, promotions, skill development.

### Work-Life Balance
Fixed hours vs flexible but demanding roles.

## Risk Considerations
- Economic downturn impact
- Skill stagnation
- Burnout

## General Guidance
- Risk-averse + stability priority → Government/stable roles.
- Growth-focused + high ambition → Private/startup.
