# Health Decision Framework

## Common Decisions
- Gym vs home workout
- Diet changes
- Surgery vs medication
- Sleep improvement strategies

## Key Criteria

### Long-Term Health Impact
Sustainability and safety.

### Physical Risk
Injury risk, side effects.

### Cost
Medical expenses, membership fees.

### Convenience
Ease of maintaining routine.

### Effectiveness
Evidence-based results.

## Risk Considerations
- Short-term extreme solutions
- Ignoring medical advice
- Overtraining

## General Guidance
- Sustainable habits > quick fixes.
- Consult professionals for major decisions.
