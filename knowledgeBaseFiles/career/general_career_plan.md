Tradeoffs:
- High income careers may increase stress.
- Stable careers may reduce growth pace.
- Specialist roles increase depth but reduce flexibility.
- Generalist roles increase adaptability but reduce niche authority.

Evaluation Prompts:
- What does success mean to me?
- Do I want stability or impact?
- How important is flexibility?
- What kind of problems excite me?

Decision Patterns:
- Early career: prioritize skill growth + exposure.
- Mid career: balance compensation with sustainability.
- Long-term planning: evaluate adaptability and automation resistance.

Risk Notes:
- Choosing based on peer pressure.
- Following trends without aptitude.
- Ignoring long-term adaptability.
- Overestimating short-term gains.
