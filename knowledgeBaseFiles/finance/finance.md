# Financial Decision Framework

## Common Decisions
- Invest vs save
- Rent vs buy house
- Loan vs self-funding
- SIP vs fixed deposit

## Key Criteria

### Risk Tolerance
Ability to handle financial volatility.

### Return Potential
Expected profit over time.

### Liquidity
Ease of accessing money.

### Debt Burden
Interest rates, repayment period.

### Long-Term Security
Retirement, emergency fund.

## Risk Considerations
- Market crashes
- Over-leveraging
- Lack of diversification

## General Guidance
- Low risk tolerance → Fixed returns.
- Long-term horizon → Market investments.
