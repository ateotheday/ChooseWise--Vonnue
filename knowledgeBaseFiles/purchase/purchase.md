# Purchase Decision Framework

## Common Decisions
- Laptop comparison
- Budget vs premium product
- Online vs offline purchase
- Upgrade vs repair

## Key Criteria

### Budget
Affordability and value for money.

### Performance
Speed, durability, specifications.

### Brand Reliability
Customer service, warranty.

### Long-Term Use
How long the product will last.

### Maintenance Cost
Repairs, upgrades, subscriptions.

## Risk Considerations
- Overpaying for unnecessary features
- Buying outdated models
- Hidden costs

## General Guidance
- Tight budget → Value-focused option.
- Heavy usage → Performance-focused.
