About ChooseWise

ChooseWise is a web-based decision support platform designed to help users make thoughtful and structured choices between multiple options.

The system allows users to define what matters most to them, assign importance to different criteria, and compare options in a clear and organized way.

To make the process easier, the platform includes an optional personality-based quiz that helps generate sensible starting weights for users who may be unsure how to begin.

The goal of the platform is simple: to turn complex decisions into clear, understandable, and confident choices.
